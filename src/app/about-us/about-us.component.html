About Us

Our Mission

At RSB Agro, our mission is to provide high-quality plants and exceptional nursery services to our customers. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable and eco-friendly gardening practices.

Who We Are

RSB Agro is a leading plant nursery located in Medirigiriya. Founded by Mr. P.K.G.R.S. Bandara, we specialize in grafting, mango plant sales, agro product sales, and handling chemicals for plant care. With a commitment to excellence, we strive to offer the best products and services to our valued customers.

Our Services

  • Grafting and plant propagation
  • Sale of high-quality mango plants
  • Agro product sales
  • Plant care chemicals and fertilizers
  • Comprehensive plant nursery management

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of skilled professionals who are passionate about horticulture and dedicated to providing exceptional customer service. Our staff includes experienced grafting specialists, knowledgeable sales personnel, and efficient administrative staff.
